    Chain Chess was one of the first games I worked on at CelleC. We released it an Android and iOS and developed it in Unity. I was working with 1 other programmer on this project. I had some hard learned lessons when working on this game from December 2020 to April 2021.

Game Jam Titles

  • Harvest Them

    Harvest Them is a 2D adventure game I made for 2021's Great Autumn Game Jam. I handled all of the design, scripting, and programming. The game is about a sole necromancer who lives in a forest that surrounds a small village. The necromancer has remained dormant for many years but has awakened to feed on the souls of the villagers.
